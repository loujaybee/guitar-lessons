
# Lesson 5 - Major / Minor

## Tone + Semitone Pattern
T = Tone
S = Semitone

Major scale - T T S T T T S

Minor scale - T S T T S T T

## G MAJOR (Ionian Mode)

```
E|-----------------------------------------2--3--
B|-----------------------------------3--5--------
G|--------------------------2--4--5--------------
D|-----------------2--4--5-----------------------
A|--------2--3--5--------------------------------
E|--3--5-----------------------------------------
```

## G NATURAL MINOR

```
E|--------------------------------------------3--
B|-----------------------------------3--4--6-----
G|--------------------------2--3--5--------------
D|--------------------3--5-----------------------
A|-----------3--5--6-----------------------------
E|--3--5--6--------------------------------------
```

RELATIVE MINOR

- E is the relative minor of G
- Minor in E is the same as major in G
