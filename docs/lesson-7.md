## A DORIAN

- Takes a bit of minor, and adds in major, with a minor seven
- If you compare it to a natural minor
- Different than natural minor in the 6th (whereas dorian has a major six)

```
E|----------------------    ----------------------5--
B|----------------------    -------------5--7--8-----
G|----------------------    ----4--5--7--------------
D|-----------------4--5-    -7-----------------------
A|-----------5--7-------    -------------------------
E|--5--7--8-------------    -------------------------
```
